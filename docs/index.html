This classifier takes ball's images from different sports as input and able to classifie 10 different types of balls as given:
1. Football
2. Basketball
3. Volleyball
4. Rugby
5. Golf
6. Cricket
7. Tennis
8. Bowling
9. Billiards
10. Baseball
You have to click on right top Classifier in the dashboard to classify any ball image.
